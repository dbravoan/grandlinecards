# Marketing Strategy: Grand Line Cards

## 1. SEO Supremacy (The "Google One Piece" Strategy)
- **Objective**: Dominate search results for Spanish One Piece TCG terms.
- **Keywords**: "Cartas One Piece", "Mazo Luffy", "Meta OP05", "Reglas One Piece TCG".
- **Tactics**:
  -   **Dynamic Metadata**: Every card page has unique Title/Desc (Implemented).
  -   **Friendly URLs**: `/cartas/op01/luffy` instead of IDs (Implemented).
  -   **Sitemap**: Auto-generated for all Cards and Decks.

## 2. Content Marketing (Pirate Academy)
- **Guides**: "Cómo jugar One Piece TCG en 5 minutos", "Guía de Colores".
- **Deck Techs**: Weekly articles functionality analyzing top decks.
- **Card Reviews**: Automatic generation of "Card Analysis" pages using data attributes.

## 3. Community Engagement
- **Deck Builder**: The core viral tool. Encourage users to share their deck links on Twitter/Discord.
- **Tournaments**: Host online tournaments (future feature) or list local events.
- **Discord Integration**: Bot that posts new top decks to a Discord server.

## 4. Social Media Growth
- **Twitter/X**: Share "Card of the Day" images generated by the platform.
- **YouTube**: Partner with Spanish OP TCG YouTubers to use Grand Line Cards as their visual aid.

## 5. Launch Plan
- **Phase 1 (Soft Launch)**: Share with local communities and Discord groups. Collect feedback.
- **Phase 2 (Content Push)**: Release 5 high-quality guides. Optimize SEO.
- **Phase 3 (Viral Tools)**: Launch "Tournament Mode" or "Collection Tracker" to retain users.
