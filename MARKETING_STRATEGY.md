# Grand Line Cards - Marketing Strategy

## 1. Executive Summary
Grand Line Cards aims to become the **#1 Resource for One Piece TCG players in Spanish**. By combining a premium deck builder, localized news, and accurate card databases, we target both competitive players and collectors.

## 2. Target Audience
-   **The Rookie**: New to TCGs. Needs "Pirate Academy", "Rules Cheat Sheet", and "Starter Deck Guides".
-   **The Captain**: Competitive player. Needs "Meta Analysis", "Deck Builder", and "Event Calendar".
-   **The Collector**: Needs "Card Database", "Price Tracking", and "High-Res Art".

## 3. SEO Strategy
### Keywords
-   **Primary**: "One Piece TCG mazos", "Buscador cartas One Piece", "Reglas One Piece Card Game español".
-   **Secondary**: "Meta OP05", "Precios cartas One Piece".

### Content Plan (The "Log Pose" Blog)
-   **Weekly**: "Meta Report" (Top decks from recent tournaments).
-   **Monthly**: "Set Reviews" (Analysis of new expansions like OP-06).
-   **Evergreen**: "Guía para principiantes" (The Pirate Academy).

## 4. Social Media Channels
-   **Twitter/X**: Rapid news, card reveals, "Card of the Day".
-   **Discord**: Community building, LFG (Looking For Game), Bug reports.
-   **Instagram**: High-quality card art showcase, specialized reels.

## 5. Growth Hacking
-   **Tournament Organizer Tools**: Provide TOs with tools to run local events easier.
-   **Influencer Partnerships**: Partner with Spanish OP TCG YouTubers for exclusive deck reveals.
